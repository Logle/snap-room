Snaproom

Snaproom allows you to create a temporary space to collaborate with anyone nearby.

You can create a room to split a check,
vote on what to eat for lunch,
play a game of backgammon,
or just chat.

Snaproom was created by Navid Karimeddiny and Steve Rubinstein.
